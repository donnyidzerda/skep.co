Tools of Titans

Tim Ferriss

The Book

Condensed notes from 200+ podcast interviews with world-class performers. Not a book you read front to back. Reference shelf material.

Broken into Healthy, Wealthy, Wise. Flip to whoever interests you. The patterns across guests are more interesting than any single interview. Everyone meditates. Everyone has a morning routine. Everyone has failed publicly and kept going.

Key Takeaways

  • Almost everyone journals
  • The most common recommended book is Sapiens
  • More than one guest said the question "what would this look like if it were easy?" changed their life
  • Most guests have a daily practice they do not skip
  • The common thread isn't talent. It's consistency and the willingness to look stupid while learning
