Extreme Ownership

Jocko Willink & Leif Babin

The Book

Everything is your fault. Not guilt. Ownership. Willink ran SEAL Team Three in Ramadi. The lessons transfer directly. No excuses. No finger-pointing.

Every chapter is a combat story followed by a business application. Sounds formulaic. Works anyway. Because the principle underneath is always the same: if you're in charge and it went wrong, that's on you. Full stop.

Key Takeaways

  • Prioritize and execute. When everything is going wrong, focus on one thing
  • Check the ego. The moment you think you have it figured out, you don't
  • Simple plans work. Complex plans fail
  • Leaders must believe in the mission. If you don't, no one under you will either
  • There are no bad teams, only bad leaders
