Antifragile

Nassim Nicholas Taleb

The Book

Resilience is overrated. Resilience just means you survive the hit. Antifragile means you get stronger from it.

Taleb is arrogant and repetitive and also completely right. This book rewires how you think about stress, volatility, and why comfort is the actual risk.

Key Takeaways

  • Small stressors build capacity. Removing all stress makes you fragile
  • The things that survive long-term benefit from disorder
  • Optionality beats prediction every time
  • Most modern systems (education, medicine, economics) are designed to be fragile
  • Via negativa — what you remove matters more than what you add
