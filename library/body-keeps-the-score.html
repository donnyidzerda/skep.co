The Body Keeps the Score

Bessel van der Kolk

The Book

Your body stores everything you never processed. That tension in your shoulders, that knot in your stomach when someone raises their voice, those patterns you can't think your way out of — they're not bugs. They're data.

Van der Kolk spent decades working with trauma survivors and this book is the result.

Key Takeaways

  • Trauma lives in the body, not in the story you tell about it
  • Talk therapy alone can't reach what's stored below the neck
  • The nervous system needs to feel safe before anything else changes
  • Yoga, EMDR, and somatic work often do more than years of cognitive therapy
  • Being "triggered" isn't weakness — it's your nervous system replaying old survival code
