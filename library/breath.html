Breath

James Nestor

The Book

You've been breathing wrong your entire life. Through your mouth, into your chest, too fast, too shallow. Nestor spent years testing what happens when you fix it. Ran experiments at Stanford, lived with freedivers, tracked down ancient breathing practices.

Blood pressure drops, anxiety drops, everything changes. And the fix is embarrassingly simple.

Key Takeaways

  • Mouth breathing at night wrecks your sleep and changes your face structure over time
  • Slow nasal breathing shifts your entire nervous system state in minutes
  • Most "anxiety" is just a breathing pattern you never corrected
  • 5.5 seconds in, 5.5 seconds out — that's the magic ratio
  • Ancient cultures all figured this out independently. We forgot it
