The Miracle Morning

Hal Elrod

The Book

Wake up early. Meditate, journal, read, affirm, visualize, exercise. Before the world gets a vote on your day. Sounds corny until you try it for 30 days straight.

The book itself is nothing special. The practice is. Elrod almost died in a car crash and rebuilt his life starting with his morning. The SAVERS framework is simple enough that you can't hide behind complexity.

Key Takeaways

  • The level of your personal development determines the level of your success
  • Your morning routine compounds. Start with 6 minutes if you have to
  • Don't negotiate with yourself in the morning. Decide the night before
  • SAVERS: Silence, Affirmations, Visualization, Exercise, Reading, Scribing. Pick your order
  • How you start the day is how you live the day. How you live the day is how you live your life
