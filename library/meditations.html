Meditations

Marcus Aurelius

The Book

Private journal of the most powerful man on earth, never meant to be published. No performance, no audience. Just a guy reminding himself every morning to not be a reactive idiot.

Written during wars, plagues, betrayals. 2,000 years old and still more useful than anything on your feed.

Key Takeaways

  • You don't control what happens. You control what you do with it
  • Most of your suffering is your opinion about the situation, not the situation
  • Do the work. Skip the credit. Repeat until dead
  • Other people's behaviour is their problem. Your reaction is yours
  • Time is the only non-renewable resource. Stop wasting it on things that don't matter
