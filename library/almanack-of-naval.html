The Almanack of Naval Ravikant

Eric Jorgenson

The Book

The operating system for wealth and happiness without selling your soul. Leverage, specific knowledge, peace. Naval's tweets and podcast clips compiled into something you can actually sit with.

Not a hustle book. Not a self-help book. More like a field manual from someone who figured out the game and then figured out the game doesn't matter.

Key Takeaways

  • Play long-term games with long-term people
  • Code and media are permissionless leverage
  • Desire is a contract you make with yourself to be unhappy until you get what you want
  • Specific knowledge is found by pursuing your genuine curiosity, not what's hot right now
  • A calm mind, a fit body, a house full of love. These things cannot be bought. They must be earned
