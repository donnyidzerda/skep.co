The War of Art

Steven Pressfield

The Book

Resistance is the thing between you and the work you know you should be doing. Pressfield names it, dissects it, and makes you feel like an idiot for ever listening to it.

Short book. You'll read it in a day. Then you'll read it again next month because resistance came back. It always does.

Key Takeaways

  • The more important the work, the more resistance you'll feel
  • Amateurs wait for inspiration. Professionals show up regardless
  • Resistance is impersonal — it doesn't care who you are. It just blocks
  • Procrastination is the most common form of resistance
  • Turning pro is a decision, not a talent. You make it once and honour it daily
