Letting Go

David R. Hawkins

The Book

The mechanism is stupidly simple. Feel the feeling. Don't label it, don't analyse it, don't push it away, don't act on it. Just let it be there until it passes. It always passes.

The whole book is basically that, said 200 different ways until it finally clicks. And when it clicks, everything gets quieter.

Key Takeaways

  • Suppressed emotions don't disappear. They run your life from underneath
  • Surrendering isn't weakness. It's dropping the resistance that's exhausting you
  • You don't think your way to peace. You feel your way there
  • Every emotion has a shelf life if you stop feeding it
  • The levels of consciousness map isn't the point. The mechanism is
