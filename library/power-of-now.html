The Power of Now

Eckhart Tolle

The Book

Presence. Ego dissolution. Tolle sat on a park bench for two years after his shift and wrote what he found there. Reads slow the first time, hits different the second.

Not a philosophy book. Not religion. Just a guy pointing at something you already know but keep forgetting. The whole thing boils down to: you are not your thoughts. And that one sentence can take a lifetime to actually land.

Key Takeaways

  • Resistance to what is creates suffering
  • The ego creates a false sense of self based on identification with thoughts
  • Presence is not concentration. It's allowing
  • The past and future only exist as thought forms. The present moment is all there ever is
  • Pain is inevitable. Suffering is the story you add to it
